Account Security

Your security is our top priority — manage your protection settings here

Change Password

A strong password is your first line of defense. We recommend using a unique password that you don't use anywhere else.

Tips for a strong password

Use at least 8 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols. Avoid using personal information or common words.

Login History

Keep an eye on your account activity. If you see something unfamiliar, change your password immediately.

New Device Alert

Get notified by email whenever your account is accessed from a new or unrecognized device.
